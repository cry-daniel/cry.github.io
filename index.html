I’m a first-year PhD student at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), supervised by Prof.Xiaoyao Liang and Prof.Zhuoran Song. My research focuses on Computer Architecture, specifically graph processing and graph neural networks (GNNs). I have also conducted research on optimizing sparse matrix multiplication and improving irregular access through prefetching techniques.
